Connect F1 TV Pro

Sign in with your F1 TV Pro account to unlock live telemetry (car speed, RPM, gear, throttle, brake, DRS) in your Homey automations.

Your credentials are sent directly to the official Formula 1 API (api.formula1.com) and are never stored by Homey or MonkeySoft. This page has no backend; the token is passed to the Homey app via postMessage and stored locally on your Homey.

Requires an active F1 TV Pro subscription.
